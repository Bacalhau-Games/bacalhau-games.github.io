Our Current Project

Dune Awakening

We are no strangers to treacherous waters and shifting tides—but now, we find ourselves in a sea of sand. As part of Funcom’s grand expedition into Frank Herbert’s Dune universe, we have spent nearly four years diving deep into the intricate systems of a highly customized Unreal Engine 5, working side by side with Funcom's team as co-developers. Our seamless integration speaks volumes about our ability to adapt, collaborate, and thrive within a large-scale production.

Like the Spice Harvesters braving the sands of Arrakis, we navigate Dune: Awakening’s complex systems with precision, ensuring performance, stability, and gameplay excellence. This was our sandwalk during this journey:

Rendering – Bringing the sands of Arrakis to life
Networking – Ensuring seamless multiplayer experiences
Server & Client Infrastructure – The backbone of stability
Performance Optimization – Pushing Unreal Engine 5 to its limits
General Gameplay & UI/UX – Crafting immersive player interactions
Production & Planning – Keeping the journey on course
